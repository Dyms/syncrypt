# Code of Conduct

Syncrypt follows the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/),
version 2.1.

In short: be respectful, assume good faith, and keep discussion focused on the
work. Harassment, personal attacks, and discriminatory behavior are not welcome.

Unacceptable behavior can be reported privately to the maintainer (contact to be
listed here before the first public release). Reports are handled confidentially.

The full Contributor Covenant text will be vendored here before the first release;
until then, this summary applies.
